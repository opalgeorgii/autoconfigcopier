IMPORTANT
- Do not move the CS2ConfigCopier.exe file
- Better to keep the folder structure unchanged
- It isn't recommended to delete the base folders:
  - cs2_cfg/
  - cs2_server_cfg/
  - csgo_cfg/

INSTRUCTIONS
----------------------------------------
Upon 1st usage:
0. Configure your Steam and CS2/CS:GO settings ONCE
1. Open CSConfigCopier.exe
2. Click on "Load" and choose primary Steam account from where your CS2/CS:GO settings will be copied (https://steamid.xyz/ to look up your SteamID)
3. Click on "Copy all Steam settings", "Copy current CS2 settings"/"Copy current CS:GO settings"
4. Save the whole folder with all the folders and the .exe file

Upon 2nd usage (reinstalled Windows/Steam/using a different account/playing from a computer club):
0. Retrieve your saved configurtation folder with CS2ConfigCopier.exe
1. Open the app
2. Click on "Paste all Steam settings" and "Paste cs2_cfg"/"Paste csgo_cfg"
3. Launch CS2
4. Open CS2 Steam properties and enable "Keep game's saves in the Steam Cloud for Counter-Strike 2"

FOLDER INFORMATION
----------------------------------------
default_configs/
Stores all default CS2 and CS:GO user-related settings.

You do not need to edit the default_configs/ folder inside cs2_cfg/ or csgo_cfg/.
The required default configs should be copied by using the program.

additional_configs/
Stores extra config files that you may want to use, replace, or remove.
Example configs are included there and will be copied to the game/csgo/cfg folder.

steam_account_settings/
This folder is created after clicking "Copy all Steam settings".
It stores Steam settings for all Steam accounts currently used on your PC.

csgo_cfg/
This folder may also contain CS:GO background files, which you can replace or remove if needed.

cs2_server_cfg/
If you use this folder, make sure all plugins and server.bat files are configured before copying.

NOTES
----------------------------------------
- If you do not need certain files or folders, you can delete them.
- However, it is best to keep the main folder structure intact and only replace or remove the files you do not want.
- Do not modify or remove default_configs/ unless you fully understand what you are changing.
